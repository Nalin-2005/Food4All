About Us

We are a team which wants to ensure food security for those who are in the need. At the same time, our motto is not to allow the food to go waste when in some pockets, food is available in surplus. To accomplish this, we plan to distribute the surplus cooked food ready for consumption from one place to other place and for those, who need the same and have no or low capacity to pay.
